About Me

Hi! I am a Munich based Data Scientist with a research background in astrophysics and particle physics.

I am an experienced software developer with a preference for Python. I currently work on developing and exploring state-of-the-art algorithms to solve real-world challenges for the Energy Transition. My technical skills are in the fields of Time Series Forecasting, Anomaly Detection, Optimization, Physical Modeling, and Scenario Simulation.

After graduating from the University of Trieste (Italy) with a master's degree in particle physics, I moved to the Technical University of Munich to pursue my PhD in astrophysics. My main scientific interest was to investigate the origin of astrophysical neutrinos using the data from the South Pole IceCube Neutrino Observatory.

I am currently working as Data Scientist at OmegaLambdaTec, a company that provides pioneering AI-powered sustainability solutions.
