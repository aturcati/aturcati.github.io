About Me

Hi! I am a Munich based Data Scientist with a research background in astrophysics and particle physics.

I am an experienced software developer with a preference for Python. I currently work on exploring and applying state-of-the-art solutions to real-life challenges in the fields of anomaly detection, physical analytics, forecasting and digital twin simulations.

After graduating from the University of Trieste (Italy) with a master's degree in particle physics, I moved to the Technical University of Munich to pursue my PhD in astrophysics. My main scientific interest was to investigate the origin of astrophysical neutrinos using the data from the South Pole IceCube Neutrino Observatory.

I am currently working as Data Scientist at OmegaLambdaTec, a company that provides pioneering smart data solutions in the sectors of energy and smart cities.
